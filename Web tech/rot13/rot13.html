ROT 13 - The Caesar Cipher

Rot 13 Encoder




Rot13 or Rot-13 (short for rotate 13) is a simple letter substitution encryption scheme. It works by replacing the current english letters in a message with those that are 13 positions ahead in the alphabet. Rot-13 encoding can be used for other purposes as well..
